ARE YOU READY?

The OWee marks the beginning of a new period in your life: your student days. But what does your student life look like?

On August 18th to the 22nd, Delft is a big party, especially for you as a first-year student. The OWee is your time to reinvent yourself and discover what really fits you.

The whole week the Bolk organises various cool activities specially for you, this way you can go all out to give your time in college an unforgettable start!

Curious what to do and what the Bolk has to offer? This website is especially built for that! Have a look around, mark the dates in your diary and visit us during the OWee.
